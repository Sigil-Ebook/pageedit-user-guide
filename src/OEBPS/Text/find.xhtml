— Searching In PageEdit —

Finding text and updating it is one of the most common tasks in PageEdit—to look for words, to remove unwanted text, or to change the formatting of specific items.

To display the Find toolbar, select the menu item Findâ†’Find or press its keyboard shortcut CTRL+F:

Actions

The buttons on the right are used to run your search commands.

  • Down Arrow: Finds the next occurrence of your search term.
  • Up Arrow: Finds the previous occurrence of your search term.
  • Match Case: If enabled, PageEdit will search for exactly what you type.
